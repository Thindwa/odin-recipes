Banana Pancakes

Description:

Banana Pancakes are a delicious and healthy breakfast option made with ripe bananas, eggs, and flour. They are fluffy, sweet, and perfect for a weekend brunch.

Ingredients:

  • Ripe Bananas
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Baking Powder

Steps:

  1. Mash the ripe bananas in a bowl.
  2. Whisk the eggs and milk together in another bowl.
  3. Mix the wet ingredients with the mashed bananas.
  4. Add the flour and baking powder to the mixture.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  6. Pour small amounts of batter onto the pan to form pancakes.
  7. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  8. Serve warm with syrup or fresh fruit.
