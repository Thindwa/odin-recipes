Chicken Stir Fry

Description:

Chicken Stir Fry is a quick and easy dish made with chicken, vegetables, and a savory sauce. It is a healthy and flavorful meal that can be prepared in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breast
  • Vegetables (Bell Peppers, Broccoli, Carrots)
  • Soy Sauce
  • Ginger
  • Garlic

Steps:

  1. Cut the chicken breast into strips.
  2. Mix the soy sauce, ginger, and garlic to make the sauce.
  3. Heat oil in a wok or large pan over high heat.
  4. Add the chicken strips and cook until browned.
  5. Add the vegetables and stir fry for 5 minutes.
  6. Pour the sauce over the chicken and vegetables.
  7. Cook for another 2 minutes until everything is well coated.
  8. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
