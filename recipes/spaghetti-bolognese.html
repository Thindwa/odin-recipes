Spaghetti Bolognese

Description:

Spaghetti Bolognese is a popular pasta dish made with minced meat and tomato sauce. It is filling, affordable, and easy to prepare, which makes it suitable for beginners.

It is a classic Italian dish that is loved by many.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Minced Meat
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onion

Steps:

  1. Boil the spaghetti.
  2. Chop the minced meat.
  3. Heat the tomato sauce in a pan.
  4. Add the chopped meat to the sauce.
  5. Cook the meat until it is done.
  6. Add the chopped onions and garlic to the sauce.
  7. Simmer the sauce for 5 minutes.
  8. Add the cooked spaghetti to the sauce.
  9. Serve the spaghetti bolognese.
